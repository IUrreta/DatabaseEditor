Performance info

The performance shown in the graphs as well as in the bar chart is the LATEST DESIGNS's car performance from that team, which does NOT mean that the parts fitted in the car are the last ones designed. Drops in performance will most likely be because of WEIGHT.
If you want to see the performance of EVERY CAR with exactly their FITTED PARTS, click on the icon.
With the lightweight parts difficulty modifier enabled, you will stop seeing drops in performance and there will be less variability in the development of the cars.
The OVERALL stat is an approximation of the car's performance, it can't be determined with 100% accuracy, and it's calculated based on the other selectable car attributes.
Please take into consideration that if you make a specific default engine not be used by any team, when selecting it again as an engine supplier, its stats will be the same as other default engine, not its original ones.
Creating a custom engine will NOT CREATE a new one in-game, it will just apply the stats that you assign the custom engine to the engines that the teams who run the custom engine use.

Forecast info

The predictions are made with an AI model trained with the developer's own save file. You can check out predictions for previous races and for the next one in the Race tab.
It is NOT ADVISED to predict the first 2/3 of the season, as the model has to take the data from those races to make predictions for the rest of the season.
In the Standings tab, you can configure a prediction for the rest of the season from the race you select in the modal. Take into account that the sooner that race is in the season, the more races it will have to simulate and it will take longer. For example, it will take much longer if you predict from the 3rd race of the season than if you predict only the last race of the season. The model will run dozens of simulations of all the remaining races and calculate the amount of times a driver has finished in every position. The darker the green is, the more chances he has to finish in that position
