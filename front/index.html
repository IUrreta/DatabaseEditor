Performance info

From the Teams tab, you will be able to edit performance from every part of any team. The number you see beside the bars are the Increment or Decrement in performance for that part in specific.
From the Engines tab, you will be able to edit directly the stats of every engine, seeing their exact performance.

Forecast info

The predictions are made with an AI model trained with the developer's own save file. You can check out predictions for previous races and for the next one in the Race tab.
In the Standings tab, you can configure a prediction for the rest of the season from the race you select in the modal. Take into account that the sooner that race is in the season, the more races it will have to simulate and it will take longer. For example, it will take much longer if you predict from the 3rd race of the season than if you predict only the last race of the season. The model will run dozens of simulations of all the remaining races and calculate the amount of times a driver has finished in every position. The darker the green is, the more chances he has to finish in that position

Comparison info

The Annotations toggle will hide or show the lines marking P1, P2, P3 and P10 in the Race form chart and Q2 and Q3 lines in the Quali form chart.
The Points progression graph has no Annotations
When comparing teams, only the Points progression graph will be available

Team editor info

The Board confidence is only editable for the player's team.
When editing the Cost cap spent, changes will not be shown in the main cost cap screen in-game, as there is no way to edit it in that window. When you design or research a new part in-game you should see changes applied in the window that appears to confirm the transaction

Important information
When you click on “Confirm”, the tool will start running dozens of simulations for each race in the championship from the one selected in the dropdown button. This means that the earlier the selected race is in the championship, the more races it will have to simulate until the end of the championship, and the prediction will take longer. Depending on your system, the simulation may take more time.
